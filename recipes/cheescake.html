Fluffy Cheesecake

Home

Description

The most fluffy and delicious cheescake you will ever have.

this recipe uses a water bath so use the water bath tutorial i have previously linked

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Graham crackers
  • sour cream
  • sugar
  • salt
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. make the crust
  2. cream the cream cheese with butter and sugar
  3. add in the restn of the Ingredients INTO THE CREAMED MIXTURE
  4. Mic the mixture, but be careful not to over mix
  5. pre heat oven to 325
  6. prepare your water bath
  7. pour batter into prepared crust
  8. put the cheescake into the over and let bake for 80 minutes
